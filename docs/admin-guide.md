- `/admin/import`

- `/admin/logs`
- View who changed what and when

## Operational Rules
- Keep `ADMIN` role limited to trusted users
- Use `STAFF_ONLY` for internal documents
- Use `HIDDEN` for records not ready for publication
- Regularly back up database and files
